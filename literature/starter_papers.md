## 📚 高优先级起点论文

### 1. 综述论文 (必收)

#### CNT 电导率综述

1. **Dresselhaus, M. S. et al. (2001).**
   - 标题：Physical properties of carbon nanotubes
   - 期刊：World Scientific
   - 数据：基础物性数据汇总
   - 优先级：⭐⭐⭐

2. **Popov, V. N. (2004).**
   - 标题：Carbon nanotubes: properties and application
   - 期刊：Materials Science and Engineering: R: Reports
   - 卷期：43(3), 75-126
   - 数据：电导率、力学性能汇总
   - 优先级：⭐⭐⭐

3. **De Volder, M. F. et al. (2013).**
   - 标题：Carbon nanotubes: present and future commercial applications
   - 期刊：Science
   - 卷期：339(6119), 535-539
   - 数据：应用导向性能数据
   - 优先级：⭐⭐⭐

---

#### CNT 力学性能综述

4. **Salvetat, J. P. et al. (1999).**
   - 标题：Elastic and shear moduli of single-walled carbon nanotube ropes
   - 期刊：Physical Review Letters
   - 卷期：82(5), 944
   - 数据：杨氏模量、拉伸强度
   - 优先级：⭐⭐⭐

5. **Yu, M. F. et al. (2000).**
   - 标题：Strength and breaking mechanism of multiwalled carbon nanotubes under tensile load
   - 期刊：Science
   - 卷期：287(5453), 637-640
   - 数据：拉伸强度实测数据
   - 优先级：⭐⭐⭐

6. **Demczyk, B. G. et al. (2002).**
   - 标题：Direct mechanical measurement of the tensile strength and elastic modulus of multiwalled carbon nanotubes
   - 期刊：Materials Science and Engineering: A
   - 卷期：334(1-2), 173-178
   - 数据：力学性能
   - 优先级：⭐⭐
